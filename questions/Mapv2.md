## III. HUMAN EXPERIENCE & ETHICAL PRESCRIPTION

### A. Emotional Regulation Vocabulary

- Fear (ਭਉ)
- Anger (ਕ੍ਰੋਧ)
- Desire (ਕਾਮ)
- Ego (ਹਉਮੈ)
